# Chapter 1: Introduction

## 1.1 Background and Motivation
Emergency Medical Services (EMS) are essential for saving lives during critical situations. However, traffic congestion and poor coordination between ambulance drivers and traffic police often delay emergency response. The Ambulance Tracking System (ATS) is designed to solve this issue by providing real-time GPS tracking and proactive alert notifications to traffic police officers.

## 1.2 Problem Statement
The project aims to develop a real-time tracking and alert system to reduce ambulance response delays caused by traffic congestion and lack of coordination.

## 1.3 Objectives of the Project
- Real-time ambulance GPS tracking
- Geospatial proximity detection
- Automated police alert system
- Secure authentication using JWT
- Interactive map visualization

## 1.4 Scope of the Project
- Real-time GPS location tracking
- Redis-based geospatial detection
- Multiple ambulance tracking
- OpenStreetMap integration
- Route visualization

## 1.5 Significance and Real-World Impact
The ATS improves emergency response efficiency by reducing ambulance delays and helping traffic police manage traffic proactively.
